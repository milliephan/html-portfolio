The Best Movies According to Millie

My top 3 movies of all time

Sprited Away

This is my favorite Miyazaki/Ghibli movie. I love the story that he creates.

The Craft

This is like a witchy, Mean-Girls-esque, 2000s perfection

Over the Garden Wall

If you want a haunting and nostalgic animation, but with cute and simple plotlines, this is the one for you.
